Germany

The population of Germany is 83 million, with the majority identifying as Christian.

Islam in Germany

As of 2019, 5.2 percent of Germans identify as Muslim. Between mid-2010 and mid-2016, Germany accepted roughly 670,000 refugees, with Muslims comprising 86% of them. In addition, 680,000 migrants were accepted into Germany from this period, with Muslims comprising 40% of them. At least half of Germany’s 16 states ban teachers from wearing headscarves, and in Hesse, the ban included civil servants. In early 2017, Bavaria prohibited full-face veils in schools, polling stations, universities, and government offices.
