About our project

This project explores Islamophobia in Germany through a variety of lenses, such as in politics, mass media, policing, and the workplace. We are students in Dr. Hatem Bazian’s ASAMST 132AC Islamophobia and Constructing Otherness course at UC Berkeley, and we selected Germany to conduct our research on Islamophobia. We would like to thank Dr. Hatem Bazian and Talha Mirza for their guidance throughout the course.
